# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Mobile phone users seeking high-quality, original AI-generated portrait wallpapers, specifically localized for Southeast Asian languages (English, Thai, Vietnamese, Burmese, Lao, Khmer).

## Product Purpose
A fast, premium, and clean gallery application to browse, filter, search, and download high-quality mobile wallpapers with seamless localization.

## Brand Personality
Apple-clean, premium, minimalist, responsive, and sophisticated.

## Anti-references
Cluttered grids, saturated gradients, generic templates, slow-loading media, and unaligned elements.

## Design Principles
1. **Apple-Grade Precision**: Details like spacing, alignment, typography, and iconography must feel premium, deliberate, and high-end.
2. **Visual Restraint**: The UI serves as a gallery frame; it must support and elevate the artwork without competing with it.
3. **Fluid Micro-Interactions**: Hover states, toggles, and transitions must feel fast, organic, and satisfying.
4. **Multi-Script Balance**: Native typography across 6 scripts must feel balanced, harmonious, and readable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High text-to-background contrast (>= 4.5:1).
- Generous touch target sizes on mobile layouts.
- Adaptive themes respecting user preferences and OS settings.
- Graceful degradation of motion for prefers-reduced-motion users.
